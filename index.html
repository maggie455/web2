'Sargaa' means creation. Nature has some of the world's best creations. Everything mankind has created till today has always been inspired by nature in some form.

Launching our collection of naturally dyed handwoven apparel, terracotta kitchenware, and wooden toys from Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.

Notify Me When We Launch

Sargaa now aims to create things which are totally derived from the woods. We work with our in-house artists and several other craftsmen across Andhra Pradesh to bring artistic lifestyle products which can be used in our daily life.

Handwoven Apparel

Naturally dyed handwoven clothing that brings traditional Indian artistry into contemporary lifestyle with earth-based materials.

Terracotta Kitchenware

Beautiful and functional terracotta kitchenware that connects you with nature while enhancing your culinary experience.

Wooden Toys

Handcrafted wooden toys that inspire creativity and imagination while being completely derived from sustainable wood sources.

At Sargaa, we make naturally dyed handwoven apparel, terracotta kitchenware and wooden toys. We focus on creating contemporary lifestyle products with earth based materials.
